Time To Get Movi'n!

Hey there fellow adventurer! With Activity Connect you can plan activities to enjoy with your friends and family! If your already familiar with the site features you can go on ahead start planning some awesome adventures, but for those who are first timers or simply need a refresher keep on reading! Since your reading this you have already created your account, you can view your account information by clicking the My Profile tab on the top right. You can browse a list of covid friendly activites that we have availible in the Browse tab. To plan an activity with friends simply click on the activity you would like and follow the instruction provided on the next page. To view your currently planned activities or to add new friends click on Social on the top navigation bar.
